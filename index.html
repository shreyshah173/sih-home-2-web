What is ayurveda?

Ayurveda, an ancient Indian system of medicine, emphasizes holistic well-being through balance in mind, body, and spirit. It incorporates natural remedies, diet, and lifestyle adjustments for optimal health.


What is the problem with the market?

The challenges faced by consumers and medical authorities include misidentification of medicinal plants, market confusion due to multiple crude drugs with the same name, and a lack of awareness about plant attributes.
